⚠️ Browser MQTT Requirement
The ESP32 publishes on port 8883 (TLS). Browsers need WebSocket MQTT. You must configure a broker that supports WebSocket (WS/WSS) — e.g. HiveMQ Cloud (WSS port 8884) or your own Mosquitto with a WS listener. The topic is auto-set to match the firmware: tasni-water/+/state
